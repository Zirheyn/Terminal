Search Engine Optimization, or SEO, is the practice of improving a website's ranking on search engine result pages to increase its visibility for relevant searches. It involves optimizing elements on a website, such as keywords, meta tags, and links, as well as off-site factors, such as backlinks from other sites. The ultimate goal is to attract more organic (non-paid) traffic to the website.
